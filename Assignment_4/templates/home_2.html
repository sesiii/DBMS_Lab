Welcome to Gram Panchayat Management

A centralized system to manage Panchayat records, employees, and citizen services efficiently.

About Gram Panchayats

Gram Panchayats form the backbone of local self-governance in India. They manage various developmental activities, public services, and welfare schemes at the village level.

  • Village Administration
  • Implementation of Government Schemes
  • Maintenance of Public Infrastructure
  • Welfare Programs and Community Development

User Roles in the System

The Gram Panchayat Management System serves multiple users:

System Administrator: Manages system operations and user access.
Panchayat Employees: Enter and update village records and schemes.
Citizens: Access village-related information but cannot modify data.
Government Monitors: Generate reports and analyze village statistics.
